Recipe #1 - Chocolate Chip Cookies


Recipe by Martha Stewart
  • Time: Prep: 20 mins
    Total Time: 1 hour 10 mins
  • Yield: around 3 dozen cookies
Ingredients Quantity
Flour 2 and 1/4 cups
Baking Soda 1/2 teaspoon
Butter 2 sticks (melted)
Granulated Sugar 1/2 cup
Brown Sugar 1 cup packed
Salt 1 teaspoon
Vanilla Extract 2 teaspoons
Eggs 2 (large)
Semisweet Chocolate Chips 2 cups

Step 1: Preheat Oven to 180 degrees celsius
Step 2: "In a small bowl, whisk together flour and baking soda; set aside"
Step 3: "In the bowl of a stand mixer fitted with the paddle attachment, beat butter and both sugars on medium speed until light and fluffy, about 3 minutes. Add salt, vanilla, and eggs; mix to combine."
Step 4: "Reduce speed to low and gradually add flour mixture, mixing until just combined. Mix in chocolate chips."
Step 5: "Using a tablespoon measure, drop heaping portions of dough about 2 inches apart on baking sheets lined with parchment paper."
Step 6: "Bake until cookies are golden around the edges, but still soft in the center, 8 to 10 minutes.Remove from oven, and let cool on baking sheet 1 to 2 minutes."
Step 7: "Transfer cookies to a wire rack and let cool completely. Store cookies in an airtight container at room temperature up to 1 week."

Recipe #2 - Blueberry Muffins


Recipe by Jordan Marsh
Published on The NY Times
  • Time: 40 mins
  • Yield: 12 muffins
Ingredients Quantity
Softened Butter 1/2 cup
Sugar 1 and 1/4 cup
Eggs 2
Vanilla Extract 1 teaspoon
Flour 2 cups
Salt 1/2 teaspoon
Baking Powder 2 teaspoons
Milk 1/2 cup
Blueberries 2 cups
Sugar 3 teaspoons

Step 1: Preheat the oven to 190 degrees celsius
Step 2: "Cream the butter and 1 1/4 cups sugar until light.""
Step 3: "Add the eggs, one at a time, beating well after each addition. Add vanilla."
Step 4: "Sift together the flour, salt and baking powder, and add to the creamed mixture alternately with the milk."
Step 5: "Crush 1/2 cup blueberries with a fork, and mix into the batter. Fold in the remaining whole berries."
Step 6: "Line a 12 cup standard muffin tin with cupcake liners, and fill with batter. Sprinkle the 3 teaspoons sugar over the tops of the muffins, and bake at 375 degrees for about 30-35 minutes.""
Step 7: "Remove muffins from tin and cool at least 30 minutes. Store, uncovered, or the muffins will be too moist the second day, if they last that long."
